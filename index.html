About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a Spanish professional football club based in Madrid. Founded in 1902, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since inception.

Real Madrid is recognized as the most successful football club in Europe with a record of 14 UEFA Champions League titles and countless domestic trophies.
